It feels great to see a post rack up thousands of likes. But if you are trying to build a serious brand, likes are largely a vanity metric. As platforms have evolved, so have the algorithms that power them.

If you want to trigger algorithmic growth in 2026, you need to look deeper into your data.

## The Big Three: Watch Time, Saves, and Shares

Algorithms want content that keeps people engaged and brings new people to the app.

1. **Watch Time / Completion Rate:** For video platforms, this is the holy grail. A video with 100 likes and an 80% completion rate will outperform a video with 1,000 likes and a 10% completion rate.
2. **Saves (Bookmarks):** When a user saves your post, they are telling the algorithm, _"This content is so valuable, I want to come back to it later."_ This heavily boosts your authority on platforms like Instagram.
3. **Shares:** Shares are the engine of virality. Content that triggers a user to send it to a friend via direct message is the fastest way to expand your reach organically.

## How to Optimize Your Strategy

Stop asking, _"How do I get more likes?"_ and start asking:

- _"Is my hook strong enough to increase watch time?"_
- _"Is this information valuable enough to save?"_
- _"Is this highly relatable enough to share?"_
