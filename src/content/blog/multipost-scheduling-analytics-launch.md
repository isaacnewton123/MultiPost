We're excited to announce two major features that our community has been requesting since day one: **Scheduled Publishing** and **Performance Analytics**. Both are available now for all MultiPost users.

## Scheduled Publishing

You can now schedule your video uploads to go live at the perfect time for each platform. No more setting alarms or manually publishing at peak hours.

### How It Works

1. Upload your video as usual
2. Select your target platforms
3. Click "Schedule" instead of "Publish Now"
4. Set the date and time for each platform (or use our recommended times)
5. MultiPost handles the rest

### Key Features

- **Per-platform scheduling** — Set different publish times for YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook
- **Smart time suggestions** — We analyze your audience's activity patterns and suggest optimal posting windows
- **Queue management** — View and manage all scheduled uploads from a single calendar view
- **Timezone support** — Schedule in your local timezone; we convert automatically

## Performance Analytics Dashboard

Understanding how your content performs across platforms has never been easier. Our new unified analytics dashboard brings all your metrics into one place.

### What You Can Track

- **Views and impressions** across all platforms
- **Engagement rates** (likes, comments, shares)
- **Watch time and retention** per video
- **Growth trends** over time
- **Platform comparison** — see which platforms drive the most results for your content

### Cross-Platform Insights

The most powerful part of our analytics is the ability to compare performance across platforms side-by-side. See at a glance where your content resonates most and adjust your strategy accordingly.
