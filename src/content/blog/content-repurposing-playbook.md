The biggest lie in the creator economy is that you need to be constantly generating brand-new ideas to feed the content machine. Trying to ideate, script, and film a unique piece of content for every platform every day is the fastest road to burnout.

The secret to infinite content isn't infinite creation; it's infinite repurposing. You need a content factory system.

## The Hero Content Strategy

Instead of making 10 mediocre short videos, focus all your energy on creating one spectacular piece of "Hero Content" per week. This could be a 15-minute YouTube essay, an in-depth podcast episode, or a comprehensive blog post.

This Hero Content serves as the foundation. Your job is now extraction, not creation.

## The Repurposing Waterfall

Once your Hero Content is done, push it through the waterfall:

1. **Chop for Short-Form:** Extract the 4 most engaging 60-second clips for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.
2. **Transcribe for Text:** Run the video through an AI transcriber. Take the best arguments and turn them into a 10-post Twitter/X thread.
3. **Reformat for Images:** Take the core lessons and turn them into a 5-slide carousel for Instagram and LinkedIn.
4. **Email the Summary:** Condense the main takeaway into a 300-word newsletter for your email list.

From one solid afternoon of work, you have just generated over 15 unique pieces of content optimized for different platforms.
