## What is MultiPost?

**MultiPost** is a SaaS platform that lets content creators upload and distribute short-form videos to **YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Facebook & more** — all from a single dashboard, in a single click.

Whether you're a solo creator, an agency, or a growing brand, managing multiple social platforms at once is time-consuming. MultiPost removes the friction out of content distribution so you can focus on what matters most: creating great videos.

### Why MultiPost?

- **Free to Start** — No credit card required. Our Free Tier is perfectly tailored for starting out.
- **Unlimited Platform Connections** — Link to every supported platform on *all* plans, including Free.
- **One Upload, Everywhere** — Write your metadata once, tweak it per-platform, and distribute it to all your connected accounts simultaneously.
- **Scheduled Publishing** — Plan your content calendar with precision across all platforms, optimizing for peak engagement times.
- **Save Time** — Forget logging into 5 different websites to upload the exact same short. Let MultiPost's parallel distribution engine handle the grunt work.
